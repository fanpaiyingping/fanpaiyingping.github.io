About Fanpie Film

反派影评 (Fanpie Film) is an independent multi-platform film media in China that focuses on the film news, reviews, as well as the industry reports around the world. It is founded in 2016 by a team consisting of directors, screenwriters, reporters and students from film and media industry, and has become one of the most influential Chinese film press within the last two years. We usually publish the content on two major platforms: (1) the weekly two-hour podcast for the serious film criticism (2) the daily updated website and WeChat Subscription (微信订阅号) for the journalistic film relevant news and reviews.
